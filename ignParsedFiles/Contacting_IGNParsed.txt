Contact IGN via the Staff Page. Issues will be forwarded to the correct party/ies. You may also report site issues (IGN, Gamespy, 1UP, etc.) and bugs to the public forum  
For billing and Prime issues, you will need to contact Customer Support.  
If you do not have a password to log-in to Customer Support, you will need to use the free-form contact form.
