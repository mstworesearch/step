Big Story
The Memory 3 - Resolve and Grief is one of 19 unlockable Memories in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. 
In order to find and view Memories, you must search for the locations based on the visual clues in your Sheikah Slate Album. Upon arriving at the correct destination, you'll find a glowing spot that will trigger the memory, unlocking it for you.
