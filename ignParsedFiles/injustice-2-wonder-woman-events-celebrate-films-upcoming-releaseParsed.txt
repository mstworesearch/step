Injustice 2 Wonder Woman Events Celebrate Film's Upcoming Release - IGN

Injustice 2 is celebrating the upcoming Wonder Woman film with special in-game events.
Players will be able to unlock Wonder Woman-themed content based on the DC film. From now until June 5, those playing the console version of Injustice 2 can earn the gear Wonder Woman wears in the movie by participating in the multiverse event "To End All Wars."
