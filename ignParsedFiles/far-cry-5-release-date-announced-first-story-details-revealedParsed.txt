Far Cry 5 Release Date Announced, First Story Details Revealed - IGN

After a week of video and art teases, Ubisoft has finally revealed more about Far Cry 5, including when it will be available.
Far Cry 5 will hit PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and PC on February 27, 2018. With development led by Ubisoft Montreal, Far Cry 5 will allow players to explore the entire open-world in-game version of Montana alone or in two player co-op.
