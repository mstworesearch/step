Big Story
In The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, stables are large tents that are found along roads scattered throughout Hyrule. 
They each offering a place to sleep, register horses, cook, purchase crafting ingredients from Beedle, and obtain directions, weapons, and Side Quests.
