The page contains the index of all the secondary missions and tasks available in Mass Effect: Andromeda. 
There are three main classifications of operation to undertake in the Heleus Cluster: 
These are side missions that are of personal importance to Ryder and the crew of the Tempest. Completing them will strengthen the bond between your crew. 
These side missions can be found around the various worlds, space stations and settlements that Ryder and their crew visit throughout the Andromeda galaxy. 
These additional tasks can be received in a variety of locations, and usually only take a few moments to complete. Some are more involved, occasionally required an ongoing search around a planet.
