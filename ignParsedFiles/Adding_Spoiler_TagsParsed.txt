Big Story
This is a brief tutorial on how to add spoilers to IGN's wikis. Spoilers are details that reveal outcomes of stories, plots, events and fates of characters in a television show, movie, book or videogame. 
Oftentimes, spoilers can be handled with a simple announcement at the top of a page using the "Red Box": 
For short, single-sentence spoilers, you may want to use the fancy spoiler tags. If you believe something on our wikis requires the spoiler tag, please use it. Please note that placing the spoiler tag all over a page will result in an ugly page.
