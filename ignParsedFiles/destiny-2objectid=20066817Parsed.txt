Big Story
Sequel to the 2014 shared-world shooter, Destiny 2 was officially announced via Destiny's official social media accounts on the 27th of March 2017 through the release of three promotional pictures. 
 


Destiny 2 is slated for a September 8th release on Xbox One and Playstation 4. Destiny 2 will also be releasing on PC but a September 8 release date has not been confirmed. 
Humanity's last safe city has fallen to an overwhelming Cabal invasion force led by Ghaul, the opposing commander of the opposing Red Legion. He has stripped the City's Guardians of their power and forced the survivors to flee.You will venture to mysterious, unexplored worlds of our solar system to discover an arsenal of weapons and devastating new combat abilities. To defeat the Red Legion and confront Ghaul, you must reunite humanity's scattered heroes, stand together, and fight back to reclaim our home. 
 


Destiny 2 is the highly anticipated sequel to Bungie's 2014 title, Destiny. Not much is known about Destiny 2 as of yet as it was only officially announced on the 27th of March 2017, but rumours have been swirling around for quite some time. 
 


Below you'll find a collection of information based on what we know for sure (facts) and what we're speculating and hearing about (rumours). They will be labeled accordingly.
