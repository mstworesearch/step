Big Story
Red Dead Redemption 2 is the upcoming sequel to the hit open-world Western action-adventure game Red Dead Redemption and its predecessor, Red Dead Revolver. 
 


Red Dead Redemption 2 was officially announced October 18, 2016 for PS4 and Xbox One. 
In addition to single player, Red Dead Redemption 2 will also "provide the foundation for a brand new multiplayer experience."
