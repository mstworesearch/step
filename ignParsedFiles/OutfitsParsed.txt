Big Story
There are several Outfits found in Horizon: Zero Dawn. These represent your armor, and each one has a different effect on your stats. Below is a list of all the armor types, and where to find them. 
This basic armor can be found early on. All three tiers of it provide you with varying levels of elemental resistance. 
This is stealth armor and wearing it will make it harder for enemies to detect you. 
This armor is a basic damage resistance armor, that will offer varying levels of melee damage resistance. 
This armor set is the most basic in the game. It offers no special bonuses, but has more mod slots than other armor. 
This armor, like it's name suggests, provides a high defensive bonus against Freeze damage. 
This armor is designed to give a high resistance to Electric damage. 
This is another resistance armor, this time providing a high resistance to Corruption. 
This armor is designed to give you a high resistance to Fire damage. 
This armor type is designed to give you a higher resistance to ranged damage. 
This is a set of legendary armor. To unlock it, you will need to find all of the Power Cell Locations. This armor, once unlocked, gives you a massive damage resistance bonus.
