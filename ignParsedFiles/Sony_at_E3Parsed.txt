Big Story
 


The Sony at E3 section collects the history and highlights of Sony's E3 (Electronic Entertainment Expo) press conferences, games and show floor displays. Sony entered the home gaming market in 1995, coinciding with the first E3. Sony has been a key player in every subsequent Expo, introducing three consoles, two handhelds, and a bevy of other products.
