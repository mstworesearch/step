Steve Aoki Headlines TwitchCon: The Party, New Attractions Announced - IGN

TwitchCon announced a slew of new attractions for its 2016 event, including TwitchCon: The Party headlined by DJ Steve Aoki.
In addition to The Party, TwitchCon also has Inclusivity City featuring "AnyKey, Hack Harassment, Online SOS, and I Need Diverse Games." Designed to help make Twitch "a welcoming place for everyone," Inclusivity City also hosts a series of Super Smash Bros. tournaments presented by Smash Sisters.
