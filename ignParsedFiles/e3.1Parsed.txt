Big Story
E3, also known as the Electronics Entertainment Expo, is the video game industry's largest annual business event. E3 typically takes place in early summer in the Los Angeles Convention Center. The show was first held 1995. E3 2014 marked the expo's 20th anniversary. 
E3 2017 takes place from June 13-15 in Los Angeles, California. 
E3 was not open to the public until 2017. Games industry professionals such as publishers, developers, and the media along with the general public are invited to the convention. Attendance numbers are in the mid ten-thousands. For more details, check Attendance and Stats.
