How to Buy a House - The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild Wiki Guide - IGN

Big Story
In The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, it's possible to own your very own home! To do so, you must travel to West Necluda into Hateno Village. 
Just to the West of Myahm Agana Shrine, look for a small footbridge behind the Bolson Construction Homes to find an abandoned house with men working to demolish it. 

Speak to Bolson - the man in pink pants, and tell him you want to buy the house.
