Big Story
The Princess Zelda is one of the confirmed Characters in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. Little is known about Zelda at this time, and more information will be added as it is revealed. 
Very little information is known about Zelda, other than that she is presumably the one that awakens Link from his slumber at the start of the game.
