Big Story
The Quest, Robbie's Research, is one of the Side Quests in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. It can be started by speaking to Purah at the Hateno Ancient Tech Lab in Hateno Village, and then journeying to the Akkala Region to find Robbie at the Akkala Ancient Tech Lab.
