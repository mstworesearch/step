NBA Playgrounds: Switch Owners to Get Free Copy of Shaq Fu: A Legend Reborn - IGN

Nintendo Switch owners who purchase, or have already purchased, NBA Playgrounds before online play is added will receive Shaq Fu: A Legend Reborn for free when it's released this fall.
Developer Saber Interactive revealed the offer ahead of an impending update to the game, with more details to come about how players can acquire Shaq Fu for free.
Challenge Mode, which allows players to compete online, has already been added to the PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and PC versions of the arcade basketball game. Developer Saber Interactive also recently released the game's first title update on PlayStation 4 and PC, adjusting the "entire shooting system" and adding 15 new players, including Shaq. The update is expected to hit Xbox One and Switch "within days."
