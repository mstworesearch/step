Vasu - The Legend of Zelda: Oracle of Seasons Wiki Guide - IGN

Big Story
The only jeweler in the world of Holodrum, he appraises the secret powers of rings made from magical seeds. Vasu also has two pet snakes which can transfer rings from one version of the game (Oracle of Seasons in this case) to the opposite version of the game via secret codes. He is located in Horon village in the Oracle of Seasons version of the game.
