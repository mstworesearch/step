Big Story
Esports are competitive tournaments held around a variety of video games, played at both a professional and an amateur level. While some game genres are more represented, such as FPS, MOBA and RTS, almost all multiplayer games have some level of Esport representation. IGN will be bringing you the latest in Esport news and tournament information for Australia and New Zealand, as well as covering off on the largest international competitions.
