Inspired by the comic book by Robert Kirkman, The Walking Dead game spans two "seasons" featuring 5 episodes each and a one-off episode between seasons called 400 Days. 
In Season 1 you play as Lee Everett, a professor and convicted murderer who was about to serve a life sentence when the zombie apocalypse happened. Lee encounters a little girl by the name of Clementine, whom he must keep safe. Click on an episode below to watch videos and read about the major decisions and their outcomes for each episode.
