Alien: Covenant Steelbook and Six Film Collection Exclusive Reveals - IGN

Believe it or not, the Alien franchise is almost 40 years old. With the May, 19th release of Alien: Covenant, the franchise now has six films total (no, the AVP films do not count), the four Ripley-centric installments and Scott's two prequels.
With that in mind, there are several new home releases on the horizon. Take your first look at the Alien: Covenant Steelbook, and two versions of the Alien collection.
