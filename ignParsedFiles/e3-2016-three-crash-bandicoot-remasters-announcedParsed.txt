E3 2016: Three Crash Bandicoot Remasters Announced  - IGN

Today at Sony's E3 2016 press conference the company announced that Crash Bandicoot, Crash Bandicoot 2: Cortex Strikes Back, and Crash Bandicoot: Warped will all be remastered and released for PlayStation 4. Activision still owns the Crash Bandicoot rights, with the remasters coming thanks to a special arrangement between Sony and the publisher.
The news came alongside the announcement that Crash will be an exclusive character in the PlayStation 4 version of Skylanders: Imaginators.
No release date or sneak peek of the remasters was given on stage.
