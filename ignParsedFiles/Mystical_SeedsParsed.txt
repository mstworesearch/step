Big Story
Mystical Seeds are what you put inside your Satchel. They grow on the Mystical Trees scattered throughout the land. They can be cut down from the trees or simply found under plants and rocks. 
* Ember Seeds: These are basically fire Seeds. Use them to burn items, light troches or defeat flammable enemies.
