Big Story

The Maku Tree is a character (well a tree, but you get my point) in the Zelda game Oracle of Seasons and Ages. The Maku Tree is the protector of Holodrum. Also the Maku Tree can be found in the northeast part of Horon Village. Also the Maku Tree in Holodrum is male but the Maku Tree in Labrynna is female.
