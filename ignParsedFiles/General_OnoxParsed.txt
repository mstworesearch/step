Big Story
Onox, the General of Darkness, is the main antagonist of the Legend of Zelda: Oracle of Seasons. By throwing the seasons into chaos in the land of Holodrum, he sets out to cause damage to the land to light the Flame of Destruction, a necessary step to revive Ganon.
