Big Story
Anything done in a wiki can be undone -- it's easy! You have the power to undo the edits of others. But with great power comes basic politeness and a bit of responsibility! 
Hit HISTORY in the top right of any page to view and/or edit history of that page. Here you can see the editor who made the change and when the change was made. On the right are three tools: 
 
Pro Tip: Click Wiki Tools in the Nav and then click Recent Changes to see a history page for the entire wiki! 
When you undo another user's change, you are given the option to leave a note -- please do so, and explain why you made the change. It's common courtesy. Unexplained or unreasonable undo actions may be reverted and the account which spawned such edits will be banned.
