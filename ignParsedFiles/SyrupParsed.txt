Big Story

Syrup is the owner of Syrup's Potion Shop which is located in the Sunken City on the way to the fourth dungeon through Mt. Cucco. It is only accessible in winter as the frozen lake along with piled up snow allows for the only access to the shop. Syrup makes a business of brewng and selling potions which of course Link can purchase. She is also the grandmother and mentor of Maple
