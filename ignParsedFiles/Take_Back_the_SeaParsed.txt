Big Story
Take Back the Sea is one of the Side Quests in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. It is started at Lurelin Village, located on the far East side of the Faron Region. 
Speak to Sebasto who is found patrolling the village often on the west side by the farthest hut.  He directs Link to eliminate all enemies at an encampment on Aris Beach southeast of the Shai Utoh Shrine in the Faron region.
