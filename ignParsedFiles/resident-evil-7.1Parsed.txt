Big Story
Resident Evil 7 Biohazard is the next addition to the Resident Evil franchise, drawing from the survival horror roots of the series while being the first Resident Evil game to put the player in the first person perspective. The game is set after the events of Resident Evil 6, taking place in modern day and revolving around a foreboding, derelict plantation mansion in rural America. Players must explore and survive the horrors within the mysterious, sprawling estate.
