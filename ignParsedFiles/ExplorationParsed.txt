Big Story
Mass Effect: Andromeda features an assortment of new tools to facilitate exploration. Here are a few we know about so far. 
The Nomad Scout Rover is equipped with interchangeable four- and six-wheel drive, thrusters, and a booster for navigating all kinds of terrain. The Nomad also has a shield that, when destroyed, emits a shield blast. This is an area of effect shockwave that'll knock nearby enemies back. 
In order to further facilitate exploration the Nomad also has a mining computer and mining drones to find and retrieve resources.
