Big Story
Dragon parts are some of the rarests and most powerful monster drops in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. 
There are three dragons who only appear at certain times of day and figuring out their drops is a little tricky. 
You can only obtain one one of four items per encounter depending on where your arrow lands: 
- Aim for the body for Dinraal's Scale, Farosh's Scale, Naydra's Scale- Aim for the claws for Dinraal's Claw, Farosh's Claw, Naydra's Claw- Aim for the fangs for Dinraal's Fang, Farosh's Fang, Naydra's Fang- Aim for the horns for Shard of Dinraal's Horn, Shard of Farosh's Horn, Shard of Naydra's Horn 
Dragon parts are used in cooking, shrine quests, armor upgrades, and traded for rupees with any of the merchants.
